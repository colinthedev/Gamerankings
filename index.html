Game Rankings

This is a list based on personal preference's ranking games ive played through-out the years.

Mario Kart 8 Deluxe

Pros:

  • Great graphics
  • Fully customizable character and vehicle options
  • Online play

Cons:

  • Requires cursing when things go wrong, and things almost always go wrong
  • Races can seem short with 3 laps
  • Red shells are the devil

Crash Bandicoot N.Sane Trilogy

Pros:

  • Puzzle like game-play
  • Enough levels to keep you busy for a very long time
  • Corky story-mode

Cons:

  • Some levels are extremely hard and frustrating
  • No online or two player game modes
  • Cut and dry type of game

Overwatch 1

Pros:

  • Teamwork aspect is a blast when you dont have a troll
  • Multiple game modes
  • Ranking based system pushes you to improve

Cons:

  • Steep learning curve for some characters
  • Plenty of trolls
  • Requires players to select certain characters in order to increase victory chance

League of Legends

Pros:

  • Loot system based on performance
  • Tons of champion options
  • Huge community

Cons:

  • Tons of trolls
  • In-app purchases
  • Addicting

World of Warcraft

Pros:

  • Best MMO ever
  • Constantly launching expansions
  • Massive open world environment

Cons:

  • Paid subscription
  • Some players get way too tilted
  • PVE can require scheduled time slots every week

Starcraft

Pros:

  • Single player and multiplayer options
  • Free online play
  • Ranking based system

Cons:

  • The most triggering game ever made
  • The most stressful game ever made
  • This is the hardest game ive ever played
